About Saf-T Child

Saf-T Child is a child safety device that is designed to save a child's life in an emergency situation. Founded by Lorraine Forest after learning of a terrible accident, Saf-T Child is designed to ensure no child is left in a car, unattended, again. The device component sits discreetly underneath any carseat, and detects when a car is in motion and in use, or turned off and unattended. If a child is detected in a carseat inside of a car that is turned off for 60 seconds, primary contacts are notified via an SMS text message. After, if the child is not moved to saafety, a larger range of secondary contacts are notified. If the child remains in the seat after a total 3 minutes unattended, local emergency services are contacted.
